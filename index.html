Surah Yaseen – Details

Surah Number 36
Revealed In Mecca (Makki Surah)
Number of Verses (Ayahs) 83
Number of Rukus 5
Parah 22 & 23
Main Theme Faith in the Qur’an, the Prophethood of Muhammad ﷺ, and the Resurrection.
Virtues Known as the “Heart of the Qur’an”. Reciting Surah Yaseen brings peace, forgiveness, and blessings.
Famous Reciters Qari Tareq Muhammad, Qari Abdul Basit, Mishary Rashid Alafasy, Saad Al-Ghamdi

Frequently Asked Questions

Surah Yaseen is often referred to as the “Heart of the Qur’an” due to its profound themes of faith, the Qur’an, the Prophethood, and the Hereafter.

Surah Yaseen has 83 verses (ayahs).

It is a Makki Surah, revealed in Makkah before the migration to Madinah.

It is believed to bring peace, forgiveness, blessings, and ease of difficulties. Many Muslims recite it daily for spiritual benefits.

Because it summarizes the central message of the Qur’an: belief in Allah, Prophethood, and the Hereafter.

It is often recited in the morning, before important tasks, and during hardships for blessings and guidance.
